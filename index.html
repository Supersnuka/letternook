Tap adjacent letters — sideways, up-down, or diagonal — to trace a 5-letter path, just like Boggle. Submit it and get Wordle-style feedback on how close it is to the hidden word tucked into this grid:

right letter, right spot

Tap a tile already in your path to rewind to that point. Letters confirmed absent get crossed out on the grid. The grid always hides a few extra real words alongside the answer — up to 3 decoys — so tracing any path that spells something real is worth a shot even if it isn't the solution. There's one official LETTERNOOK a day — 6 guesses, every guess must be a real word, same puzzle for everyone, and it's saved automatically. Use Practice for unlimited extra rounds that don't affect your streak.

Scoring: solve with no hints for 3 points, 1 hint for 2 points, 2 hints for 1 point. Solve on your first guess for a +2 efficiency bonus, second guess for +1 — stacks with your hint score. Points add up in your Total Points stat.

Your solve time is tracked for fun (shown when you finish, plus your personal best) — it's never scored and there's no countdown while you play.
